Overview

Purpose

Are you the ‘out-doorsy’ type? The ‘Neighborhood social’ type? Green thumbed Gardiner? Lawn Enthusiast? The fun Mom? The cool Dad? Or are you the type that loves dealing with obnoxious and annoying mosquito bites? One of the biggest draw backs to being in the yard is dealing with the bug bites. Dealing with rashes, allergic reactions, and your kids scratching themselves, complaining, and staying inside all summer? Mosquito Free Zone is a company dedicated to mosquito elimination, customized to your backyard. We guarantee 95-100% mosquito reduction. We deliver fast and efficient results. No toxic fumes, no restrictions on what you can and can’t do with your home. Our products are kid and pet friendly, and our easy-to-use website and app make it easy to schedule an appointment, pay, and receive feedback from our licensed and trained technicians on your personalized treatment, allowing you to tell us where the problem-areas are and make special requests. Don’t let those blood suckers keep you from being the King of your backyard this summer. Take charge and call Mosquito Free Zone today. In charge of your backyard.

Audience

Our audience is specifically those who will be in their backyards and spend time outside. Middleage and upper class families with kids, grandkids, and those who want social gatherings at their homes. Those that want to protect their families, allow people to enjoy their time outside this summer and not be impeeded by bug bites, specifically by mosquitos. Images will be focused on families and summer fun. They will be focused mainly on Kids having fun and playing outside, Women gardening and playing with their kids and watching their husbands play with their kids or work, and husbands/men mowing the lawn and grill looking very manly, happy, in charge, and powerful. But with Dad bods. Advertising will focus on women with families seeing as they will be the biggest response group but will also be rolled out to men in a more rugged work centered ad. Age demographic for our customers will be from 30-55. Manily married women acting as homemakers.
