We are a company based here in Rexburg Idaho. Our founder gained a knack and skill for mosquito abatement during his time working for other pest control establishments and thought he could do better. Our devotion as a company is to make your experience with your own yard as enjoyable as possible. With our 100% Satisfaction Guarentee, our Price-Match Guarantee, and our Unbeatable Customer Service We strive to make the most out of your experience with our company.
